Reception

Effortless patient registration.

Register patients in seconds. Instantly create visits, capture biodata, and trigger workflows across every department — all without paper.

  • Instant patient search and deduplication
  • One-click visit creation with department routing
  • Realtime queue management for all staff
Doctor Module

Precision clerkship and diagnosis.

An intuitive interface built for clinicians. Record vitals, clerk patients, and code diagnoses using the full ICD-10 database — all from one screen.

  • Structured clerkship with history templates
  • Full ICD-10 searchable diagnosis database
  • Direct lab requests and e-prescriptions
Triage & Vitals

Serial vitals tracking that matters.

Monitor patient vitals over time with automatic trend charts. Triage staff can record, compare, and flag abnormal readings in real time.

  • BP, temperature, pulse, SpO₂, weight tracked over time
  • Visual trend charts per admission
  • Triage priority assignment built-in
Patient Dashboard

Complete patient history at a glance.

Every visit, diagnosis, prescription, and lab result — organised chronologically in one view. No more hunting through paper files.

  • Full medical history timeline
  • Instant patient search across all records
  • Secure role-based access control
