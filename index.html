Streamlined Patient Flow

Eclinic HMS provides a clear, step-by-step process from patient arrival to departure, ensuring a smooth and efficient experience for both staff and patients.

  • Reception registers the patient and creates a visit.
  • Doctor clerks the patient, making requests for labs or prescriptions.
  • Realtime notifications are sent to relevant departments (Lab, Pharmacy).
  • Cashier handles all billing and payments seamlessly.
  • All actions are logged for reporting and analytics.

Efficient Clerkship and Diagnosis
